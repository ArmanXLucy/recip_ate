Seven Layer Beet Salad Recipe

Ingredients

For the Salad Layers:

  • 3 medium beets, roasted, peeled, and sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens (arugula, spinach, etc.)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted and chopped

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast the Beets: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wrap the beets in foil and roast for about 45-60 minutes, or until tender. Allow to cool, then peel and slice thinly.
  2. Prepare the Salad: In a large serving dish or individual bowls, layer the mixed greens, roasted beets, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and walnuts.
  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  4. Dress the Salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad just before serving. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve: Serve the salad immediately, garnished with extra feta cheese and walnuts if desired.

Tips

  • Preparing the Beets: Beets can be roasted in advance and stored in the refrigerator. This makes it easier to assemble the salad when you're ready to serve.
  • Variations: Add other vegetables like bell peppers, carrots, or avocado for more variety. You can also substitute the feta cheese with goat cheese for a different flavor.
  • Nuts: Toasting the walnuts enhances their flavor and adds a nice crunch to the salad. Be sure to let them cool before adding to the salad.
  • Make Ahead: You can prepare the components of the salad ahead of time and assemble them just before serving. Keep the dressing separate until ready to serve.
