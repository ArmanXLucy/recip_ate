BBQ Jackfruit Sandwiches Recipe

Ingredients

For the Jackfruit:

  • 2 cans young green jackfruit in water, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4-6 burger buns
  • Coleslaw (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Jackfruit: Cut the drained jackfruit into smaller pieces, discarding any seeds and tough cores.
  2. Cook the Aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened and fragrant, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the Jackfruit: Add the jackfruit pieces to the skillet. Stir in the smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the BBQ Sauce: Pour the barbecue sauce over the jackfruit mixture, stirring well to coat. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the jackfruit is tender and flavors are well combined.
  5. Shred the Jackfruit: Using a fork, shred the jackfruit to resemble pulled pork. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
  6. Assemble the Sandwiches: Toast the burger buns if desired. Pile the BBQ jackfruit onto the buns and top with coleslaw, if using.
  7. Serve: Serve the sandwiches warm and enjoy!

Tips

  • Jackfruit Selection: Use young green jackfruit in water or brine, not in syrup. The former has a neutral flavor suitable for savory dishes.
  • Customize Your Sauce: Feel free to adjust the amount of BBQ sauce to taste, or add extra spices for a kick.
  • Make Ahead: The BBQ jackfruit mixture can be made ahead of time and reheated, making it perfect for meal prep.
  • Serving Suggestions: Serve with a side of sweet potato fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal.
