Grilled Chimichurri Portobellos Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chimichurri Sauce:

  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Portobellos:

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Make the Chimichurri Sauce: In a bowl, combine parsley, cilantro, garlic, olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, and red pepper flakes. Stir well and season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Mushrooms: Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Brush the portobello mushrooms with olive oil on both sides and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the Mushrooms: Place the mushrooms gill-side down on the grill. Cook for 5-7 minutes per side, or until tender and slightly charred.
  4. Serve: Transfer the grilled mushrooms to a serving plate. Spoon the chimichurri sauce generously over the top. Serve warm.

Tips

  • Choosing Mushrooms: Select large, firm portobello mushrooms for grilling. Smaller mushrooms can also be used, but adjust grilling time accordingly.
  • Chimichurri Variations: Customize your chimichurri with different herbs or add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra freshness.
  • Serving Suggestions: Serve the grilled portobellos as a main dish with a side salad or as a topping for burgers and sandwiches.
  • Make Ahead: Chimichurri sauce can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to a week.
