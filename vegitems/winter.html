Winter Grain Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

For the Grain Bowl:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cooked farro
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1 cup kale, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/4 cup roasted pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Roast the Vegetables: On a baking sheet, toss the sweet potato and Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20-25 minutes, until tender and slightly crispy.
  3. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the Bowl: In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and farro. Add the roasted vegetables, kale, pomegranate seeds, and pumpkin seeds.
  5. Dress the Salad: Pour the dressing over the grain bowl and toss to combine. Top with feta cheese, if using.
  6. Serve: Serve warm or at room temperature. Enjoy your hearty and nutritious winter grain bowl!

Tips

  • Customizable Grains: You can substitute the quinoa and farro with other grains like brown rice or barley.
  • Add Protein: Add grilled chicken, tofu, or chickpeas for additional protein.
  • Make Ahead: Prepare the grains and vegetables ahead of time for a quick and easy meal during the week.
  • Seasonal Variations: Swap out vegetables based on what's in season, like butternut squash or beets.
