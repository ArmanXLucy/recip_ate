Crispy Rice Salad Recipe

Ingredients

For the Crispy Rice:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt, to taste

For the Salad:

  • 1 cup mixed greens (arugula, spinach, etc.)
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh herbs (cilantro, mint, basil), chopped
  • 1/4 cup toasted nuts (almonds, cashews, etc.), chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Crisp the Rice: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the cooked rice and spread it out in an even layer. Let it cook undisturbed for about 5-7 minutes, or until the bottom is golden and crispy. Stir and cook for another 2-3 minutes until evenly crispy. Season with salt and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper, avocado, fresh herbs, and toasted nuts.
  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lime juice, soy sauce, honey, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Assemble the Salad: Add the crispy rice to the salad bowl and drizzle with the dressing. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve: Serve the salad topped with crumbled feta cheese, if desired. Enjoy immediately for the best texture.

Tips

  • Using Day-Old Rice: Day-old rice works best for making crispy rice as it is drier and less sticky. If using fresh rice, spread it out on a tray and let it cool and dry out a bit before frying.
  • Variations: You can add other vegetables like cherry tomatoes, radishes, or carrots. For protein, consider adding grilled chicken, tofu, or shrimp.
  • Adjust the Dressing: Taste the dressing before adding it to the salad and adjust the seasoning to your preference. You can add more lime juice for acidity or honey for sweetness.
  • Serving Suggestions: This salad is best served immediately to keep the rice crispy. If you have leftovers, store the components separately and combine them just before serving.
