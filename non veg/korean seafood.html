Korean Seafood Pancake (Haemul Pajeon) Recipe

Ingredients

For the Pancake:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1 1/4 cups cold water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed seafood (shrimp, squid, clams), cleaned and chopped
  • 1/2 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small zucchini, julienned
  • 1 red chili pepper, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Make the Batter: In a large bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, rice flour, cold water, egg, salt, and black pepper until smooth. The batter should be slightly runny.
  2. Add Ingredients: Stir in chopped green onions, mixed seafood, sliced onion, julienned zucchini, and sliced red chili pepper (if using) into the batter.
  3. Cook the Pancake: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Pour half of the batter into the skillet, spreading it evenly to form a pancake.
  4. Fry Until Golden: Cook for 3-4 minutes until the bottom is golden brown and crispy. Carefully flip the pancake and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes on the other side.
  5. Repeat: Remove the cooked pancake from the skillet and repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more oil if necessary.
