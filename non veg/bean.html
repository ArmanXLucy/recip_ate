Mutton with Black Bean Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

For the Mutton:

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons fermented black beans, rinsed and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Mutton: In a bowl, mix the mutton pieces with soy sauce and cornstarch. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix the mashed black beans, oyster sauce, sugar, and chicken broth. Set aside.
  3. Stir-Fry the Mutton: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the mutton and stir-fry until browned. Remove and set aside.
  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add the garlic, ginger, onion, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the Sauce: Return the mutton to the skillet. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir well. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and coat the mutton.
  6. Season and Serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with rice.

Tips

  • Marinate for Tenderness: Marinating the mutton helps to tenderize the meat and infuse flavor.
  • Adjust the Spice: Add some chili flakes or fresh chilies if you prefer a spicier dish.
  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh ginger and garlic enhance the flavor profile of the dish.
  • Control the Saltiness: Rinse the black beans thoroughly to control the salt level in the sauce.
