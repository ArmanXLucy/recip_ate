Dill Lime Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lime, sliced for garnish
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, lime juice, dried dill, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the Chicken: Brush the marinade over the chicken breasts, coating them evenly. Let the chicken marinate for at least 15 minutes or up to 1 hour for more flavor.
  3. Preheat the Grill: Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Place the marinated chicken on the grill or skillet. Cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through and has nice grill marks.
  5. Rest the Chicken: Remove the chicken from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with lime slices and fresh dill. Serve hot with your choice of sides.

Tips

  • Marinating Time: For a more intense flavor, marinate the chicken for longer, ideally overnight.
  • Pairing Suggestions: This dish pairs well with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables.
  • Oven-Baked Option: You can also bake the chicken in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 20-25 minutes if you prefer.
  • Additional Flavor: Add a pinch of cayenne pepper to the marinade for a spicy kick.
