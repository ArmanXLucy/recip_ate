Hoisin Baked Ribs Recipe

Ingredients

For the Ribs:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs (about 4 pounds)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

For the Hoisin Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha or chili sauce (optional for spice)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 300째F (150째C).
  2. Prepare the Ribs: Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs for better tenderness. Season the ribs with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  3. Wrap the Ribs: Wrap each rack of ribs tightly in aluminum foil and place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake the Ribs: Bake in the preheated oven for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the ribs are tender and the meat easily pulls away from the bone.
  5. Make the Sauce: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine hoisin sauce, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sriracha, garlic, and ginger. Stir and cook until heated through.
  6. Baste and Broil: Remove ribs from the oven and carefully unwrap them. Increase oven temperature to 400째F (200째C). Brush ribs generously with hoisin sauce.
  7. Broil the Ribs: Place ribs back in the oven and broil for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce is sticky and caramelized. Keep a close eye to prevent burning.
  8. Serve: Remove from oven, let rest for a few minutes, and then slice between the bones. Serve hot with extra sauce on the side.

Tips

  • Remove the Membrane: Removing the membrane from the ribs ensures they are tender and flavorful.
  • Marinate for More Flavor: For even more flavor, let the ribs marinate in the dry rub overnight in the refrigerator.
  • Adjust Spice Level: Adjust the amount of sriracha or add more chili sauce to taste for a spicier kick.
  • Use a Meat Thermometer: A meat thermometer can help ensure your ribs are cooked to perfection.
