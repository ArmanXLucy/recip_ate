Mutton Kosha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into medium pieces
  • 4 large onions, sliced
  • 6-7 green cardamoms
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 4-5 tomatoes, pureed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Garnish: Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Marinate the mutton with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt for at least 2 hours.
  2. Heat oil in a thick-bottomed pot. Add cumin and coriander seeds, let them splutter. Add cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, and bay leaves.
  3. Sauté the sliced onions until golden brown.
  4. Add the marinated mutton to the pot and cook until the meat changes color.
  5. Add tomato puree, cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add garam masala and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add enough water to cover the mutton and cook until the meat is tender.
  8. Adjust the consistency of the gravy as per your preference.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use bone-in mutton.
  • Adjust the spice level according to your taste.
  • You can use a pressure cooker to cook the mutton faster.
  • Serve hot with rice or roti.
