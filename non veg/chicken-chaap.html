Chicken Chaap Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 kg chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seed paste
  • 1 tablespoon cashew nut paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mace powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup fried onions

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a large bowl, mix the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, poppy seed paste, cashew nut paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala powder, nutmeg powder, mace powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat the ghee and vegetable oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook on medium heat for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add Fried Onions: Add the fried onions to the pan and mix well. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for another 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Adjust Seasoning: Check the seasoning and adjust salt if needed. The ghee and oil should separate from the gravy when the chicken is done.
  5. Serve: Serve the Chicken Chaap hot with naan, paratha, or steamed rice.

Tips

  • Marinate Longer: Marinating the chicken for a longer time enhances the flavor and tenderness.
  • Use Whole Spices: Whole spices ground fresh will provide a better aroma and flavor.
  • Fried Onions: Use store-bought fried onions or make them at home by deep-frying thinly sliced onions until golden brown.
  • Slow Cooking: Slow cooking on low heat allows the flavors to meld and the chicken to become tender.
