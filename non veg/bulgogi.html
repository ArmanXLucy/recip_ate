Bulgogi Mutton Recipe

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 pound mutton, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 small onion, grated
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, chopped

For Cooking:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a large bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated onion, grated ginger, rice wine, black pepper, red pepper flakes, sesame seeds, and chopped green onions.
  2. Marinate the Mutton: Add the sliced mutton to the marinade, ensuring all pieces are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  3. Cook the Mutton: Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated mutton to the skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes, or until the meat is browned and cooked through. Remove the mutton from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Cook the Vegetables: In the same skillet, add sliced onion, julienned carrot, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Combine and Serve: Return the cooked mutton to the skillet with the vegetables. Stir well to combine and heat through. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Tips

  • Meat Choice: Ensure the mutton is sliced thinly for even cooking. If you prefer, you can use beef instead.
  • Marinade Time: Longer marination enhances flavor. Aim for at least an hour or marinate overnight if possible.
  • Vegetable Variations: Feel free to add other vegetables like mushrooms or zucchini to your taste.
