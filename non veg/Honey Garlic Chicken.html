Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar or rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Garnish:

  • Sesame seeds
  • Chopped green onions

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken thighs or breasts with salt and pepper on both sides. In a shallow bowl, combine the flour and cornstarch. Dredge each piece of chicken in the mixture, shaking off any excess.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (75°C). Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate.
  3. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds, until fragrant. In a small bowl, mix together the honey, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, and red pepper flakes. Pour the sauce mixture into the skillet and bring to a simmer.
  4. Thicken the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon of water with 1 teaspoon of cornstarch to create a slurry. Add the slurry to the skillet and stir well to combine. Let the sauce simmer for 1-2 minutes until it thickens.
  5. Combine and Serve: Return the chicken to the skillet and coat it well with the sauce. Let it cook for another 1-2 minutes to ensure the chicken is heated through and fully coated in the sauce. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions. Serve hot with steamed rice or vegetables.

Tips

  • Chicken Options: You can use either chicken thighs or breasts. Thighs tend to be juicier, while breasts are leaner.
  • For Extra Flavor: Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, garlic, and a little honey for 30 minutes before cooking.
  • Vegetarian Option: Use tofu instead of chicken. Cube the tofu and cook it in the same manner for a similar dish.
